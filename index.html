About

Welcome to my research repository. I am a professor at the H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology. My research focuses on simulation methodology, Monte Carlo methods, steady-state quantile estimation, and statistical computing. My work includes collaborations with researchers from institutions worldwide, particularly in the areas of stochastic processes and variance reduction techniques. Feel free to download and cite any of my work.
